About cat

Cats can be divided into domestic cats and wild cats. Humans have a long history of raising cats, which can be traced back to 10000 years ago. In modern times, cats have become one of the most widely used pets in the world. Cats hunt other animals by ambush, and most of them can climb trees. There is a fat meat pad at the bottom of the cat's toes to avoid making a noise when walking and not startling the rat when hunting. When traveling, the claws are in a retracted state to prevent the claws from being blunt and will stretch out when catching rats and climbing.

Cats are quick and good at jumping. Eat fish, rats, rabbits, etc. The reason why cats like to eat fish and mice is that cats are nocturnal animals. In order to see things at night, they need a lot of taurine, while mice and fish contain taurine. Therefore, cats eat it not only because they like to eat fish and mice, but also because of their own needs.

The cat can walk and jump lightly on the high wall as if nothing had happened.It can't help but be impressed by its sense of balance. This is mainly due to the cat's outstanding response nerve and sense of balance. It only needs to slightly change the position and height of the tail to achieve the balance of the body, and then use the strong muscles and strong joints of the back feet to jump quickly. Even if it falls at a high altitude, it can change its body posture in the air and land lightly and accurately. Good at climbing, but not good at falling from the top. Even when falling or jumping from a height, the cat adjusts its balance by its tail so that its padded limbs touch the ground.
